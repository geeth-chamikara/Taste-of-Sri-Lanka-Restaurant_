Food Culture & Heritage

Sri Lanka is the home of True Cinnamon. Our cuisine is defined by roasted curry powder, black pepper, cardamom, and the aromatic curry leaf.

Rice and Curry is the staple. It is traditionally eaten with the right hand to mix the textures and flavors of the dhal, sambols, and curries together.

During the Sinhalese & Tamil New Year, tables are filled with Kiribath (milk rice), Kavum (oil cakes), and Kokis (crispy rosettes).
